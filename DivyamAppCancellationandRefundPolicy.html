Divyam App Cancellation and Refund Policy

Ricaria Support Services Pvt. Ltd., the owner and operator of the Divyam App, provides this Cancellation and Refund Policy for your reference.

Free Services:

  • Live Darshan: Viewing live darshan of temples through the Divyam App is entirely free and does not involve any cancellation or refund policy.

Paid Services:

  • Online Puja Booking:
  • Non-Refundable: Due to the nature of the service, puja bookings once confirmed are non-refundable. This includes both Group Pujas (Samuhik Pujas) and Exclusive Family Pujas.
  • Exceptions: We may consider a refund on a case-by-case basis if:
  • The puja cannot be performed due to unforeseen circumstances on our end.
  • There is a technical error during the booking process that results in a duplicate booking.
  • Chadhava/Offering:
  • Non-Refundable: Once a chadava/offering is booked for a temple, it is non-refundable.

Reasoning for Non-Refundable Policy:

Our puja and chadava services involve coordinating with priests at various temples across India. Once a booking is confirmed, resources and preparations are made to conduct the puja or offering on your behalf.
