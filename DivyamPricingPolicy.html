Divyam Pricing Policy

Ricaria Support Services Pvt. Ltd. is proud to present Divyam, your one-stop app for experiencing the divine. Divyam offers a free live darshan feature for famous temples across India, allowing you to connect with your spirituality from anywhere. Additionally, Divyam facilitates online puja bookings, donations, and offerings, making it easier than ever to participate in religious practices.

Free Features:

  • Live Darshan: Enjoy live streams of various renowned temples in India, bringing the spiritual experience directly to your device.

Paid Services:

Divyam offers a range of services to enhance your spiritual journey. Here's a breakdown of our pricing:

1. Online Puja Bookings:

  • Samuhik Puja (Group Puja): Participate in a puja ceremony conducted for a collective group. This is a cost-effective way to fulfill your religious obligations.
  • Price: ₹ 901 (inclusive of 20% commission)
  • Exclusive Family Puja: Have a puja performed specifically for your family and their well-being.
  • Price: ₹ 9,501 (inclusive of 20% commission)

2. Chadhava/Offerings:

  • Temple Donations: Donate to your chosen temple directly through Divyam. We offer a hassle-free way to contribute for various causes supported by the temple.
  • Commission: 20% on the total donation amount.
  • Custom Offerings: Offer puja samagri (religious items) or other specific items to deities at your chosen temple.
  • Commission: 20% on the total offering amount.

Important Notes:

  • The commission fee on paid services covers the operational costs and facilitates the smooth functioning of Divyam's platform.
  • All prices are listed in Indian Rupees (INR).
  • Divyam reserves the right to adjust pricing based on market conditions and service offerings.
