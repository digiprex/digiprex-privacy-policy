Divyam App Shipping Policy

Order Processing:

  1. General Information: All puja videos are delivered to consumers digitally upon the successful completion of the puja. These pujas are performed physically at a designated location by our experienced priests.
  2. Delivery Method: Puja videos will be delivered directly to the email address / WatsApp number provided during the booking process. Please ensure your email address / WatsApp Number is correct to avoid any delays.
  3. Video Quality: We strive to deliver high-quality video recordings. However, the quality may vary based on various factors, including the internet connection at the puja location.
  4. Issues with Delivery: If you do not receive your puja video within the expected timeframe, please contact our support team at info@divyamapp.com, and we will address the issue as soon as possible.

Shipping Time:

  1. Our commitment is to provide the recorded puja video to you within 24 - 48 hours of the puja being conducted. In the case of any delay due to unforeseen circumstances, you will be promptly notified.
